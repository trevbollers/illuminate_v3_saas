### What Is NEVER Paywalled

These features stay free forever — learn from GameChanger's mistake:

- Basic schedule viewing
- RSVP / availability
- Game results and scores
- Family dashboard (basic view)
- Age verification status
- Team chat (basic)
- Event registration (the league pays, not the parent)

**Rule**: Parents should never have to pay to see where their kid's game is or what the score was.

## Partner Tier — Uniform Partner Integration

### The Model

```
TRADITIONAL MODEL (competitor apps)
────────────────────────────────────
Team pays $9.99/mo for app
Team separately buys uniforms from random vendor
No connection between the two
Platform gets: $9.99/mo
Uniform vendor gets: full margin

GO PARTICIPATE PARTNER MODEL
──────────────────────────────
Team commits to uniform partner
    → Gets Pro features for $4.99/mo (50% off)
    → Gets 15–25% off uniform retail pricing
    → Platform gets: $4.99/mo + rev share from partner
    → Partner gets: guaranteed volume + customer data
    → Team gets: cheaper software + cheaper uniforms = clear win

NET EFFECT FOR TEAM:
    Without partner: $9.99/mo software + full-price uniforms
    With partner:    $4.99/mo software + discounted uniforms
    Savings: $60/year on software + $200–500 on uniforms
```

### Revenue Flow

```
UNIFORM ORDER: $2,000 (15 players × full kit)
    │
    ├── Retail price:        $2,500 (what they'd pay elsewhere)
    ├── Partner tier price:  $2,000 (20% discount)
    │
    ├── Partner keeps:       $1,600 (COGS + margin)
    ├── Go Participate gets: $400   (20% rev share on order)
    │
    ├── Plus monthly SaaS:   $4.99/mo × 12 = ~$60/year
    │
    └── Total GP revenue per team/year:
        Partner tier: $60 (SaaS) + $400 (rev share) = $460
        vs.
        Pro tier:     $120 (SaaS) + $0 (no partner) = $120

        Partner tier generates 3.8× more revenue per team
```

### Stickiness Mechanics

```
WHY TEAMS STAY ON PARTNER TIER:
    │
    ├── Switching cost: lose uniform discount
    ├── Reorder convenience: sizes on file, one-click reorder
    ├── Budget predictability: known pricing each season
    ├── Integrated experience: sizes collected in-app, status tracked
    └── Coach time savings: no shopping around for vendors

WHY PARTNER STAYS WITH GO PARTICIPATE:
    │
    ├── Guaranteed volume: every partner team = guaranteed order
    ├── Zero acquisition cost: GP sends them customers
    ├── Data advantage: team sizes, reorder timing, growth trends
    ├── Embedded ordering: frictionless purchase flow
    └── Multi-year relationships: teams reorder every season
```

## MidAmerica 7v7 Specific Economics

As the launch league tenant, MidAmerica 7v7 has a unique position:

```
MIDAMERICA 7v7 AS LEAGUE TENANT
─────────────────────────────────

League tier: $79.99/mo (may be comped/discounted as launch partner)

Revenue MidAmerica 7v7 generates FOR Go Participate:
    │
    ├── Teams forced to create GP accounts to register
    │   └── Conversion funnel: Free → Pro or Partner tier
    │
    ├── Registration fee processing
    │   ├── Example: 64 teams × $450/event × 3% GP fee
    │   └── = $864 per event in processing fees
    │
    ├── Player verification volume
    │   ├── 64 teams × 12 players avg = 768 verified players
    │   └── Each player now portable to OTHER leagues on GP
    │
    └── Network effect
        ├── 768 families now have GP accounts
        ├── Those families are on OTHER teams too
        └── Those other teams discover GP → organic growth

REVENUE MIDAMERICA 7v7 GETS FROM Go Participate:
    │
    ├── Better registration experience → more teams sign up
    ├── Reduced admin burden → less staff time on paperwork
    ├── Age verification handled → fewer eligibility disputes
    ├── Professional event management → premium perception
    └── Your Prep Sports coverage → marketing/exposure value
```
